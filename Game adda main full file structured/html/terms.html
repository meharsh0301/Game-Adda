1. Membership Agreement

1.1 Membership Eligibility and Requirements

To become a member of Game Adda, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Age Requirement: You must be at least 16 years of age. Members between 16-18 years old must provide written parental consent and have a parent or legal guardian co-sign the membership agreement.

1.2 Membership Types and Benefits

Game Adda offers several membership tiers, each with distinct benefits and privileges:

  1. Basic Membership: Includes 10 hours of gaming per month, access to standard gaming equipment, and participation in member-only events.
  2. Premium Membership: Provides unlimited gaming hours, priority access to high-end equipment, 15% discount on food and beverages, and early registration for tournaments.
  3. VIP Membership: Offers all Premium benefits plus exclusive access to VIP lounges, personal locker, monthly game credits, and invitation-only events.

Membership at Game Adda requires payment of recurring fees according to the following terms:

  • Non-Refundable: Membership fees are non-refundable except as required by law. No refunds will be given for partial months of service.
