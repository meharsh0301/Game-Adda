Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Game Adda ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our gaming center, use our website (www.gameadda.com), mobile application, or participate in our events and services.

1. Introduction

At Game Adda, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, processing, and protection in compliance with applicable data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

By accessing or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect various types of personal information to provide and improve our services to you:

  • Identification Data: Full name, date of birth, government-issued ID (for age verification), photograph
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, physical address
  • Financial Information: Payment card details, billing address, transaction history (processed securely via PCI-compliant processors)
  • Membership Details: Membership tier, join date, membership number, loyalty points
  • Gaming Profiles: Usernames, avatars, gaming preferences, skill levels
  • Event Participation: Tournament history, rankings, team affiliations

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your interactions with our services:

  • Gaming Activity: Games played, duration, scores, achievements, in-game purchases
  • Facility Usage: Equipment used, time spent, preferences, booking history
  • Digital Interactions: Website visits, app usage, clicks, page views, search queries
  • Social Features: Friends lists, chat logs (monitored for inappropriate content), team communications

2.3 Technical Data

For security and analytics purposes, we collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, browser type
  • Network Data: Connection type, ISP, approximate location (city-level)
  • Performance Metrics: Load times, error reports, crash analytics
  • Security Data: Login attempts, suspicious activity logs

2.4 Special Category Data

In limited circumstances, we may process:

  • Health information for accessibility accommodations
  • Biometric data for secure facility access (with explicit consent)
  • Age verification data for compliance with gaming regulations

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following lawful purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Create and manage your membership account
  • Process payments and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Provide access to gaming equipment and facilities
  • Enable participation in tournaments and events
  • Deliver customer support and technical assistance

3.2 Personalization

  • Recommend games based on your preferences
  • Customize your gaming experience and interface
  • Tailor promotions and special offers
  • Create personalized training programs

3.3 Communication

  • Send service-related announcements (membership updates, policy changes)
  • Provide event invitations and tournament details
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Send marketing communications (with opt-out option)

3.4 Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and gaming experiences
  • Conduct market research and player surveys
  • Optimize facility layout and equipment allocation

3.5 Security and Compliance

  • Prevent fraud, cheating, and unauthorized access
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Protect the rights and safety of our community

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

Processing Purpose Legal Basis
Contractual Necessity Processing required to fulfill our membership agreement and provide requested services
Legitimate Interests Processing necessary for our business operations, security, and service improvement
Legal Compliance Processing required by law (e.g., age verification, tax reporting)
Consent Processing based on your explicit permission (e.g., marketing communications)

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Service Providers

We engage trusted third parties to assist with operations:

  • Payment Processors: Secure transaction handling (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Cloud Services: Data storage and infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics Providers: Usage tracking and business intelligence (Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Platforms: Email delivery and campaign management (Mailchimp)
  • Game Publishers: Limited data sharing for tournament verification

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when legally required:

  • In response to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process
  • To establish or exercise our legal rights
  • To defend against legal claims
  • To investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change and outline your choices.

5.4 Community Features

Certain information may be visible to other community members:

  • Gamer tags and avatars in leaderboards
  • Tournament results and achievements
  • Public forum posts and comments

6. International Data Transfers

As a global gaming community, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We implement appropriate safeguards including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Privacy Shield certification for US-based providers
  • Data processing agreements with all international vendors

7. Data Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

7.1 Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access

7.2 Organizational Measures

  • Strict access controls and role-based permissions
  • Comprehensive employee training on data protection
  • Incident response plan and breach notification procedures
  • Regular privacy impact assessments

Despite these measures, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to promptly addressing any vulnerabilities.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined:

Data Category Retention Period
Account Information 5 years after last activity
Financial Records 7 years for tax compliance
Usage Data 3 years from collection
Marketing Preferences Until consent withdrawal
Inactive Accounts 2 years after last login

Data may be retained longer when required by law or for legitimate business needs such as dispute resolution.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

9.1 Access and Portability

Request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

9.2 Correction

Update inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

9.3 Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

9.4 Restriction

Limit processing of your data while we verify accuracy or address objections.

9.5 Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

9.6 Consent Withdrawal

Revoke previously given consent at any time.

9.7 Complaint

Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below. We may require identity verification before processing requests.

  • We implement age verification measures
  • Parental controls are available for minor accounts
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Parents may review or delete their child's information
