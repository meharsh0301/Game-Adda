Our Story

From Passion to Profession

Founded in 2018 by a group of gaming enthusiasts, Game Adda started as a small gaming cafe in Mumbai with just 10 PCs. What began as a passion project quickly grew into one of India's most recognized gaming destinations.

Our founders, all competitive gamers themselves, saw the need for a professional yet welcoming space where gamers of all levels could come together, compete, and grow.

Today, we operate 5 state-of-the-art gaming centers across India, host national tournaments, and have built a community of over 50,000 registered members.
